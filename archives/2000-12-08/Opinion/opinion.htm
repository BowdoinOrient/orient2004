    As an employee of Bowdoin, the wife of a Brunswick Police Officer, and a member of this community, I question the motives of anyone who refuses to leave the scene after a police officer asks him to. Even in small-town America, police officers put their lives on the line everyday. When my husband leaves for work each day, I can only hope and pray that he returns safely. When placed in every different possible situation, he has to be on his guard. On the one day he lets his guard down to "innocent" curiosity seekers, he will not return.
