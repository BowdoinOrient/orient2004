Museum jazzed up by exhibit
JONATHAN PEREZ, STAFF WRITER
Jazz Seen, an exhibit on display at the Walker Art Museum, is an attempt at translating the feelings and emotions behind jazz music into a wide array of visual and cultural imagery. It was put together with the input from Associate Professor of Music James McCalla, who uses the it as a source in his class History of Jazz II. [read the article]
