April 13, 2001
Volume CXXXII, Number 21

Bowdoin women rock the lounge

   Bowdoin women brought down the house this past Thursday, when VAGUE and Miscellania combined their talents in their 2nd annual joint performance in Morell Lounge of Smith Union. The dancing ranged from hip-hop to ballet, and the crowd was treated to Miscellania's performance of songs such as Dido's "Thank You." Smith Union was filled to near capacity with an energized crowd.
