A true community effort
By Erich Buschmann '01

    I would like to publicly thank everyone involved in the second fall Invitational for Students of Color. The entire Bowdoin College community willingly came together in an attempt to fulfill the goals of the board of trustees: to increase diversity on campus. First, I would like to thank all of the students who hosted one or more students for three nights. I would like to thank every host for being a fine representative of the college. While the hosts were the most visible contributors to the weekend, there were many people behind the scenes that allowed the weekend to run smoothly without glitches. The list is long because it was a campus effort this past weekend and if I accidentally leave anyone out, I apologize in advance.
