Monday

John Donovan Lecture
Scott Lasensky, a specialist in American foreign policy and the Middle East, will give a talk titled "The Collapse of the Middle East Peace Process and the Future of U.S.-Israeli Relations." Lasensky will discuss the U.S.-Israeli relationship and the challenges the U.S. will face given the continuing violence between Israelis and Palestinians. He will also offer long-range predictions concerning the future of the U.S.-Israeli relationship.
Druckenmiller Hall, Cleaveland 151, 7 p.m.
