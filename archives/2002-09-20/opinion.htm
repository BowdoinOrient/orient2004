Calling the U.N.'s bluff on Iraqi policy
PATRICK ROCKEFELLER, COLUMNIST
For months, like poker players trying to win a hand while holding nothing, the chattering classes of professional Bush critics had been calling for the President to go to the UN to get approval for a strike on Iraq, thinking either that he never would or that he'd fail to be convincing if he did. Bush called their bluff, and was holding aces. [read the article]
