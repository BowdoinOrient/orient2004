February 9, 2001
Volume CXXXII, Number 15

Students work to brighten library's temporary look

ORIENT STAFF

   Hawthorne-Longfellow Library held an open house Wednesday in which students worked with Mark Wethli, the A. LeRoy Greason Professor of Art, to paint a mural on one of the temporary construction partitions in the reference area. The mural was an attempt to cover up the library's "under construction" atmosphere and was designed by 26 students enrolled in Painting 1. In addition, library staff gave tours of some completed staff space and showed students plans and color boards for the renovation project.
