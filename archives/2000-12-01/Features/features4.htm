December 1, 2000
Volume CXXXII, Number 10

Physicist to address "scary" questions

   Have you ever wondered about black holes, the Big Bang, Einstein's Theory of Relativity, or any other outrageous science question? If you have, then Edwin F. Taylor's physics colloquium is a must.
   Taylor is the Senior Research Scientist Emeritus in the Department of Physics at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Professor Taylor will deliver a talk entitled "The Universe: Questions You Were Afraid to Ask" on Wednesday, December 6 at 4:30 p.m. in Searles Lecture Hall, Room 315. Come with any question you have about the physical world.
   So if you ever wondered if we could travel forward or backward in time, then remember December 6 at 4:30 p.m. in Searles Room 315.
