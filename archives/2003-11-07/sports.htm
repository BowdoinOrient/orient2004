UConn basketball is a way of life for fans
NICK LAROCQUE, STAFF WRITER

When winter hits, the neighbors, who have spent the rest of the year playing an everyday watered-down version of the famed Boston/New York rivalry, declare a temporary ceasefire that is symbolized by the "UCONN Huskies" flags that appear outside their homes.
