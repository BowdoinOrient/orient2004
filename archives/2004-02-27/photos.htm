Mark Yakavonis '05 puts the pressure on his opponent. Yakavonis' teammates Kyle Petrie '06 and T.J. McLeod '04 led the team against Bates on Saturday with 17 and 13 points, respectively. (Hans Law, Bowdoin Orient)

 

Bowdoin fights for possession in front of the net. The team clinched a pair of wins this weekend over Wesleyan and Trinity. The team has a much-needed weekend off. (Hans Law, Bowdoin Orient)
