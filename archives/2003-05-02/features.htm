Public transportation?
ANDREAS SEIFERT, FACULTY CONTRIBUTOR
I have to admit that I am spoiled. However, before I came to the U.S. I did not know that. Back in Germany, even in my hometown of Neuwied, which is not much bigger than Brunswick, we actually have means of public transportation. Hourly buses connect Neuwied with every little surrounding village, and trains come and go from the bigger cities almost every fifteen minutes. I took all of that for granted and even complained that there were no buses running between 10:00 p.m. and 5:00 a.m. [read the article]
